Chiyuki 知幸

About:

She is known as the Balck-Haired Woman (Kurokami no Onna 黒髪の女 ), her real name is Chiyuki which has 2 kanji Characters (chi 知) means to know, wisdom and (yuki 幸) means happness. Chiyuki is amnesic woman who works at Quindecim as Decim’s assistance.
